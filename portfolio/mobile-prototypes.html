Portfolio: Mobile Prototypes

In mid-2020, I was invited to enrol in a startup generator programme being run by the global venture capital firm, Antler. Throughout the programme, I got the chance to work with a bunch of talented individuals on my own business ideas and theirs.

One of the ideas that I worked on was for a PFM (or Personal Financial Management) app for women - an idea from the very talented Lisa Tsai.

She invited me to participate in a design sprint for her idea, and within a day we had planned out an early iteration of what she was hoping to achieve. The idea has since evolved away from this initial plan, so I thought it would be a good idea to publish the primary artefact I delivered out of that design sprint, here in my portfolio - a mobile app prototype that I put together from scratch for an app we called DreamCatcher.
